Muscle Building Foods for Gaining Lean Muscle

1. Eggs

Eggs contain high quality protein, which could help benefit muscle building and recovery.

  • Proteins are made up of amino acids, which are the building blocks of protein
  • Eggs contain large amounts of the amino acid leucine
  • Important for maximizing protein synthesis for muscle gain

Eggs also contain a range of other nutrients that could help build muscle:

  • Phospholipids
  • Omega-3 fatty acids
  • Cholesterol
  • Vitamin D
2. Chicken

Chicken are often considered a staple for gaining muscle because they're packed with protein. Each 3-ounce (85-g) serving contains about 26.7g of high quality protein.

  • Contains generous amounts of B vitamins niacin and B6
  • Helps body function properly during physical activity
  • Promotes fat loss while increasing muscle mass
  • Supports muscle strength development
3. Greek Yogurt

Dairy contains a healthy mixture of fast-digesting whey protein and slow-digesting casein protein.

  • Contains double the protein of regular yogurt
  • Ideal post-workout snack
  • Beneficial before bed due to protein mixture
  • Supports muscle recovery and growth
4. Tuna

Tuna contains nearly 20g of protein per 3-ounce (85-g) serving. It also has high amounts of vitamin A and several B vitamins.

  • Rich in B12, niacin, and B6
  • Provides omega-3 fatty acids
  • Supports muscle health
  • Helps prevent age-related muscle loss

Key Points for Muscle Growth:

  • Consume 1.6-2.2g of protein per kg of body weight daily
  • Eat every 3-4 hours to maintain protein synthesis
  • Include complex carbs for energy and recovery
  • Stay hydrated with at least 3-4 liters of water daily

Remember to focus on whole foods and maintain a slight caloric surplus for optimal muscle growth.
